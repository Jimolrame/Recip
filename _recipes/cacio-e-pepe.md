ingredients:
- 120g long pasta
- Pecorino cheese, grated
- Pepper

directions:
- Cook pasta al dente
- Mix cheese and fresh ground pepper in a bowl
- Lift pasta in the bowl and mix
- Toss splashes of pasta cook water

---

You should toss some olive oil.
