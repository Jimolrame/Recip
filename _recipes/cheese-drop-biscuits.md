ingredients:
- 120g flour
- 1 tsp salt
- 2 tsp yeast
- 120g cheese, grated
- 170g milk

directions:
- Preheat oven to 356F. Lightly grease a tray and set aside
- Mix everything
- Form big drops on the the tray
- Bake for 20-25 min
